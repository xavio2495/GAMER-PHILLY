VALORANT

Valorant is a tactical shooting game involving two teams
with 5 players in each team. Every player can sign in and
play remotely from anywhere in the world. Every game has
25 rounds and the team which wins 13 of them first
wins the game. Player can choose their in-game
characters called "Agents" at the start of the game.
